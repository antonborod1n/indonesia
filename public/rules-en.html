Placement rules

We immediately remove vacancies that are associated with financial pyramids; ask to issue a "special" bank card for payments; use the services of guarantors; requiring the payment of funds; offer to send SMS to short numbers or call to a paid telephone line; network marketing and other dubious ways of earning; contrary to the law; contains direct and hidden advertising of goods and services; involves the provision of sexual services; require preliminary sending of documents; associated with religious sects

Each vacancy is moderated. Jobs that do not comply with these rules may be edited or blocked without notifying the client. In this case, recalculation of the cost and / or refund of funds is not made. The site administration reserves the right to refuse to publish a vacancy without explanation. If your vacancy is not on the site - write to technical support

The text of the vacancy should not contain spelling and punctuation errors, as well as unjustified abbreviations. Each block must start with a capital letter.

The information in the "Description" column should include professional requirements for the applicant and a description of the job in general terms. The vacancy will not pass moderation if the description is taken from the fields "Responsibilities" and "Required skills"

It is forbidden to copy the same text in different blocks of the vacancy announcement

  • The use of non-normative vocabulary is prohibited
  • Duplicate vacancies are prohibited
  • Employer contacts can only be indicated in the relevant sections
  • The difference between the upper and lower income level should not exceed 30%

Refund policy:

  • Funds can be returned only in one case - if you made an erroneous payment. You can request a refund within 24 hours after purchase, through technical support
  • When making a legitimate return, we will refund the full amount minus the commission. Refunds are usually processed within 10-15 business days after we receive the claim. Funds are returned to the account corresponding to the original payment method
