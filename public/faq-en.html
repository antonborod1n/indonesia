Questions and answers

How does the job search work?

To get a relevant result, you need to specify the necessary job parameters in the search, for example, skills or the name of the profession

How to set up a job search filter?

In the filter settings, for the most correct search for vacancies, you need to select a category, skills and salary range. After that, vacancies relevant to the request will appear in the feed

There are no suitable vacancies for me, what should I do?

Change the vector of searches, do not get hung up on a single position-profession. If you can’t find the “perfect” option for a long time, reconsider your search options. We love our site very much, and its efficiency is impressive. But still, you don’t have to stop at just one option. Be sure to ask acquaintances, friends and relatives if there are any vacancies in their companies

How to post a job?

To create a vacancy, you need to go to the page for filling in information about the vacancy. Registration is not required

The cost of posting vacancies

Prices for posting vacancies can be found in the tariffs section.

How long does a job moderation take?

Job moderation, as a rule, takes from several minutes to several Job moderation, as a rule, takes from several minutes to several

When will a vacancy be posted on the channel when buying a premium publication?

Immediately after the moderation of the vacancy, it is also published on the channel. But if there is a large queue, then it can take up to 5-7 days. As a rule, a vacancy on the channel is published within 24 hours after moderation.

How long are vacancies posted for?

Vacancies with any placement rate are published for a period of 30 days. After 30 days, it disappears from the site search, but the link to the vacancy will be relevant.

How to extend the term of posting a vacancy?

Unfortunately, there is no possibility to extend the vacancy. You can re-publish a vacancy by filling in all the required fields of the vacancy from scratch.

How can I find out the reason why the moderator rejected my vacancy?

If the vacancy has disappeared from the site prematurely, contact technical support to find out the reason for the removal. Technical support contacts are listed on the website, or you can write to the mail.

Why does the moderator ask for documents from my company?

This means that the moderation department needed more complete information about your company for additional verification. Specialists constantly check users, vacancies and companies to prevent fraud and misleading

Why do you need to describe the vacancy in detail?

A job description can be compared to an advertisement that is supposed to attract the right candidates. Therefore, it should be less about the company and its requirements, and more about the candidate and his needs.

Didn't find an answer or have another question? Ask via chat or technical support.
